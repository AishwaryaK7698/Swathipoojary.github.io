Who am I?

Hi there, I am Reme Le Hane and I am a Front-end Web Developer with many years of experience. My expertise primarily includes HTML5, CSS3 and Less. I have also worked with JavaScript as well as some of its frameworks, namely jQuery, Angular and Node.JS. Additionally, experience in Facebook Application Development, SEO and CMS's such as Joomla and WordPress.

Since March 2014, I am employed with TAG Worldwide as a Front-end Web Developer.TAG is a global brands service agency with a local office in Century City. I am primarily responsible for developing and maintaining clients access portals, as well as developing new functionality and features as per clients specifications.

In June 2014 I was approached to publish a beginners course on Less entitled Rapid Less through Packt Publishing, which was then published at the end of November that same year.

Publication

Back in June 2014 I was contacted via Packt Publishing to author a video course for an Introduction to Less, the CSS Preprocessor.
I must say that I was somewhat surprised, I never even considred the notion of something like this in my carrerr, I always through that the online courses I always watched were done by professionals as a full-time job.

It was a fun and at time frustrating challenge from start to finish, haivng to write scripts that were both interactive and predictive, in the sense that I had to plan the entire flow of an hour long video course with no experience what so ever.

In the end, it turned out great and I get to add another father to my belt with my own Front-End related video course, Rapid Less
